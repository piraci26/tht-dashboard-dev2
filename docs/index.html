03 Methodology

How a flip is generated.

Step 01

IQ Bands

For each ticker we compute a 20-bar simple-moving-average basis and a ±2σ envelope. A regime flip is recorded the bar a close crosses the basis — green when close > basis, red when close < basis. Pure price math, no parameters per name.

basis = SMA(close, 20) · σ = stdev(close, 20) · bands = basis ± 2σ
Step 02

IQ Oscillator momentum

IQ Oscillator measures the rate-of-change of trend by taking RSI(5) of the EMA(5) − EMA(20) differential, centred on zero. A second-derivative momentum oscillator: it flips before price often does. A longer-term WMA(RSI(close, 15) − 50, 5) line is overlaid for context.

bxt = RSI(EMA(5,c) − EMA(20,c), 15) − 50 · centred zero
Step 03

Multi-timeframe scan

Both indicators are computed independently on daily, weekly and monthly bars. Universe is 1,500+ US equities, EOD OHLCV from Yahoo Finance, refreshed every five minutes. Streak column shows how long the prior regime held before today's flip.
