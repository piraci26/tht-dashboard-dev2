THT Dual Scan — Design Concepts

Three opinionated redesigns commissioned in parallel from the design team. Each took a different research lane (editorial publication / institutional terminal / premium consumer finance), built a complete light + dark theme system, and implemented all functionality. Click any concept to preview the live build.

Current dev2 baseline lives at /tht-dashboard-dev2/. Production at /tht-dashboard/.

No. 01
Editorial · Publication

The Dual Scan, A Quant Bulletin

Treats the dashboard as a serious financial broadsheet that happens to host live tables. Serif headlines, drop caps, italic kickers, fleuron section breaks, byline-style metadata. A faded-crimson editorial accent.

Refs: Stratechery · Doomberg · Lenny's · Bloomberg Opinion · Every
No. 02
Terminal · Institutional

Trading-desk density, modernised

Maximum information density without ugliness. Status row at top with live counts, hairline borders, monospace numerics, color-coded data signals, sticky header, sparkline chips. ~38 rows visible per screen at 1080p.

Refs: Bloomberg Terminal · Koyfin · Linear · Vercel · TradingView Pro
No. 03
Spatial · Premium consumer

Approachable for the sophisticated

Generous whitespace, soft shadows, layered cards, considered hierarchy. Warm-cream paper canvas in light, near-black warm dark in night mode. Brand teal accent. The kind of tool a sophisticated client would happily pay $50/mo for.
