THT Dual Scan — Design Concepts

Three opinionated redesigns commissioned in parallel from the design team. Each took a different research lane (editorial publication / institutional terminal / premium consumer finance), built a complete light + dark theme system, and implemented all functionality. Click any concept to preview the live build.

Current dev2 baseline lives at /tht-dashboard-dev2/. Production at /tht-dashboard/.

No. 01
Apple · iOS / macOS

If Apple shipped a stock scanner

Apple Stocks app meets Apple Music. Translucent toolbar with vibrancy blur, iOS segmented controls for tabs, Apple Wallet–style layered cards, SF Pro typography, Apple's exact systemGreen / systemRed / systemBlue. Light mode is pure Apple white; dark is layered Apple Music black.

Refs: Apple Stocks · Apple Music · Apple Wallet · Apple HIG · iOS 17
No. 02
Linear · Vercel · Geist

Premium SaaS polish

Linear's pill nav with Vercel's hairline cards. Geist gradient headlines, signature `cubic-bezier(0.16, 1, 0.3, 1)` easing, violet accent, inset highlight on every surface. Dark by default with the Linear `#08090a` near-black + slight blue undertone. Light is Vercel off-white.

No. 03
Stripe · Robinhood · Notion

Friendly consumer-finance

Wealthsimple warm-cream canvas, Stripe Atlas card chrome, Apple Wallet segmented controls. Hero copy in plain English ("Stocks flipping bullish & bearish, in plain sight"). Stripe purple→cyan gradient on accent words. Inter typography. The kind of dashboard your non-finance friends would enjoy.
